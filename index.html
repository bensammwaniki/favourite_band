Boys 2 men

Boyz II Men (pronounced boys to men) also known as
B2M, is an American boy band from Philadelphia,
Pennsylvania, best known for emotional ballads
and a cappella harmonies.

Michael Sean McCary

Michael Sean McCary (born December 16, 1971)
is an American singer, known for being
a former bass singer of the R&B group
Boyz II Men (in which he was sometimes
known as Mike Bass).
In 2003, McCary left Boyz II Men

Nathan Bartholomew Morris

Nathan Bartholomew Morris (born June 18, 1971)
is an American singer, businessman, and the
founding member of American band Boyz II Men.

Shawn Patrick Stockman

Shawn Patrick Stockman is an American singer,
songwriter and record producer,
best known as a member of the vocal
group Boyz II Men. He was a judge on the
television show The Sing-Off. In addition
to Boyz II Men, Stockman was a member
of the group Black Men United.
